About Us

Save My Nation is a student community initiative to mitigate the impact of COVID-19 using technology as a tool. We are a group of 10000+ volunteers from various parts of India who primarily focus on major problems like migration, supply of material to affected workers etc. We are looking for more volunteers from various technological, social and call centre domains. We already have a help line consisting of 25+ volunteers to address problems that the government faces.

At this crucial stage, our government relies on us for support. We encourage Universities, Colleges, Student Communities, state governments, central government departments, NGO's and Tech Startups to join us. Let's work together to come up with solutions and save lives.

What do we focus on?

  • Inter-City Migration Problems

  • Inter-State Migration Problems

  • Supply Chain Model during Disaster
